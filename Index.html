Introduction to Welding

Welding is a form of chemically bonding metals or some thermoplastics together using heat.
This process usually is done to join piecess of metal together.

Welding is used all over the world,
to mend or join pieces of metal together so that they have a bigger more complex piece,
potentially as part of an even bigger project.
